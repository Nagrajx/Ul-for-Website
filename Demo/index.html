Web Designing is a creative field that requires designers to create graphics, typography as well as images that are used only on the World Wide Web. Web design can be pursued at undergraduate, certificate, and certification levels. The most common kind of web design course is certificate programmes that can be studied in online mode. Online web design courses are available for students to pursue for free or at a cost. Depending on the style and quality of the course, web design courses often take one month to two years to complete. In India, web design course fees range from INR 5000 to INR 3 lakh.
